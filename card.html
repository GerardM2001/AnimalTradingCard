Dragon

The only people in the world that can ride dragons are
those with Targareyan blood

  • Scientific Name: Draco
  • Average Length: 63 ft
  • Average Lifespan: 120 years
  • Habitat: dank caves, wild mountain reaches, sea bottoms

Dragons come in all shapes and sizes! some have beards, some have very large horns residing at the top of their heads, and some have very beautful/colorful scales on their bodies but all of them have something in common and that is the ability to fly with their very long wings thats attached to their arms.
